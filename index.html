Where Passion Meets Innovation

Like all fields, the technology within the recording arts has drastically changed. Throughout the years, we've seen the rise of great improvements such as digital audio workstations, advanced microphones, and audio interfaces.

We believe in offering prospective students not just the key to understanding and using new technology, but to think and adapt as more are introduced. It is an exciting time to record music. However, our education is not limited to the technical aspect of production. We want to help the students apply what they haved learned onto their own projects.

Programs We Offer

Student's have the option of choosing between two different Certificate Programs. Each one requiring at least 36 credits for completion.

Music Production & Engineering

The quintessential studio experience. Students enrolled in this program can expect to learn the principles of sound design, microphones, and music theory. All work will be conducted in a real studio environment using Pro-Tools. Students will have the opportunity to learn the various ways to record all instruments, and at the end of each semester- record a live band or chamber group. All studio time will be supervised by the instructor.

Electronic Production & Design

For those that are veering into the more electronic music or sound-design territory. This certificate will be more focused on MIDI applications and sampling. All work will be conducted on both Pro-Tools and Cubase digital audio workstations. At the end of each semester, students will have had the opportunity to mix and compose for various film or video game projects. All studio time will be supervised by the instructor.
